QuickFill Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

QuickFill is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how QuickFill handles data collected by the extension.

1. Data Collection

QuickFill does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data from CSV files uploaded by users is processed locally in the user’s browser.

2. How Data is Used

QuickFill only uses the data from your CSV files to populate forms on websites you select. No data leaves your device, and no third parties have access to it.

3. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab – To access the currently active webpage for filling forms.
  • storage – To save your field mappings and preferences locally.
  • scripting – To locate and populate form fields on the pages you visit.
  • Host Permissions – To interact with websites where you want to fill forms.

These permissions are necessary for the extension to function properly and are not used to collect personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

QuickFill does not use or share data with third-party services.
